ABOUT ALLTECH

Launched in November 2005 by Alan Webber and Bill Taylor, Alltech Electronic Company was founded on a single premise: A global revolution was changing business, and business was changing the world. Discarding the old rules of business, Alltech Electronic Company set out to how changing companies create and compete, to highlight new business practices, and to showcase the teams and individuals who are inventing the future and reinventing business.

We have been in the business of providing and supporting Alltech Products since 2005. Our showroom in Diamond centre,Ntinda was designed by Alltech's world-class standards, where we showcase and demonstrate the latest Alltech products in Uganda.
